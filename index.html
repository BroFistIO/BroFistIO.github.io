What is BroFistCoin?

This is the one that's really gonna take off. Trust me. I am the most trustworthy Youtuber on the platform and I'm telling you right here, right now, BroFistCoin is where it's at. We're gonna BROFIIIIIIIIIIIIIIST... our way to the TOP! This is the cryptocurrency that's really gonna make it. It's gonna be great.

BroFistCoin (PEW) is a fan made cryptocurrency powered by the Ethereum blockchain. You can send it to your friends and trade it on an exchange such as ForkDelta.

When compared with traditional cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, BroFistCoin comes with significantly reduced transaction fees and each transaction will generally confirm way faster as well (about 5-10 minutes in normal conditions).

The main purpose of BroFistCoin (for now) is to serve as an incentive for creators to come up with top quality, unique and funny memes for PewDiePie's meme review and LWIAY episodes. Whenever PewDiePie rates a meme highly in one of his videos we will make sure the author of that meme is rewarded PEW coins for their efforts.

However, because BroFistCoin lives on the Ethereum blockchain, we can now utilize the power of Smart Contracts to create all sorts of other cool things.

For example we have currently started working on a prototype of what you can call "PewDiePie's Collectibles". We're not quite there yet, but eventually you will be able to purchase virtual collectibles with your PEW coins. The first collectible will most likely be the infamous chair that can be bought for 399 PEW coins.

It's strongly recommended to read our FAQ to fully understand the concept especially if you are new to Ethereum.

And finally, since this is a project with absolutely no funding behind it, we would appreciate a donation of any kind to help us hire graphics designers, solidity developers and social media managers to really make this project awesome.
